His actions derived me too much motivation, as a result I decided to create a biography website about him.

This project is mainly focused on to single page website, however I have included two pages one page is single page website in which there are 4 sections, the section is consist of Jumbotoron with grey background with two columns in it. One column is covering Elon Musk Picture and inside second one there is paragraph with button. The second is also made up of two columns as well but medium size. One is about Elon Musk and the second has embeded video from youtube via iframe code. The third section is a gallery page with 6 pictures are in it which are license free are availble in img picture. Finally,The last section reflects Contact page with a simple contact form in it, I have also added social icons with a hover give a more nice look, and in the footer I have added Three A tag to make mail and telphone protcol with about page along side by them.
