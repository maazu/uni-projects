WHO IS ELON

THE MAN BEHIND THE WORLD OF FUTURE

Elon Musk founded the company that would become PayPal, in 1999, he netted close to $180 million from PayPal’s sale to eBay. In 2002 he launched SpaceX, a private company focused on shaking up the moribund space industry.Then a year later came Tesla Motors, a start-up car manufacturer that aimed to produce all-electric production cars, something mainstream manufacturers had tried to do and failed miserably.He is also chairman of the board and largest shareholder at the solar energy developers SolarCity, and recently made world news by publishing design studies for a solar-powered ‘Hyperloop’ rapid transit system, capable of reaching Birmingham from London in eight minutes.
